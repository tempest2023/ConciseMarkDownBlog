# About Me

My name is Tempest (Tao Ren).

I am a Senior Full-stack Software Engineer, startup founder, and researcher working across AI agents, full-stack product systems, and structure-aware machine intelligence.

I joined [Newfront](https://www.newfront.com/) in October 2025 after leaving TikTok, and I currently work on internal insurance AI systems as a Senior Full-stack Software Engineer. My recent work centers on the Placement AI Agent initiative, agent-assisted workflows, high-trust product UX, and production software for complex broker operations. On January 27, 2026, [WTW completed its acquisition of Newfront](https://investors.wtwco.com/news-releases/news-release-details/wtw-completes-acquisition-newfront), bringing Newfront's technology-enabled broking model and AI platform into WTW's broader strategy.

In summer 2025, I co-founded [Tira AI](https://tira.tempest.fun) with Sam Liu, a UC Berkeley alumnus. We explored AI-native execution products, received top-10% feedback in YC S25, and built traction through conversations with founder programs and early-stage investors including PearX, Berkeley SkyDeck, Techstars, Antler, 500 Global, Beta University, and others, with a subset of those conversations progressing to preliminary investment interest.

I also enjoy building fast, ambitious products around emerging interfaces. Most recently, I built [AgentShelf](https://github.com/tempest2023/AgentShelf), a GEO Readiness Console that uses Generative UI to make agent-user interaction visible, inspectable, and operational for commerce teams.

Previously, I worked at TikTok on social and user relation features, at Ansys on digital twin tooling, and at Ant Group / Alibaba on large-scale financial product systems.

At **[Ant Group](https://www.antgroup.com/en/)** and [Alibaba](https://www.alibabagroup.com/en-US/), I worked in mobile technology on low-code financial product systems, dynamic templates, and live-streaming components. One representative product surface was **[Wealth Shop](https://medium.com/alipay-and-the-world/how-alipay-changed-the-way-china-invests-and-helped-a-fund-grow-400-times-over-9c13f77af4b6)**, where I helped build software that supported financial institutions at meaningful scale.

That mix of startup execution, product engineering, and research is what shapes how I build today.
